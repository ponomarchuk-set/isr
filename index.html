Index of Social Responsibility (ISR)

Evidence-based democracy for communities, organizations, and DAOs.

What is ISR?

ISR is a platform that transforms group decision-making. Unlike one-person-one-vote systems, ISR calculates a dynamic influence score for each member, combining expertise, contribution, and contextual relevance to the voted issue. Every vote reflects the true wisdom and responsibility of your community.

How It Works

  • Dynamic Microcommunities: Relevant stakeholders automatically gathered for each issue—directly, not by static representation.
  • Matrix of Values: Community-defined core values used to assess contribution and build social karma.
  • Weighted Voting: Member influence adapts by proven expertise, history, and real engagement.
  • Transparency: All actions logged and influence calculated—responsibility and privacy balanced by design.

The Philosophy

Inspired by Hannah Arendt's political theory, ISR aims to restore authentic participation and collective agency in the digital world. We create space for plurality, new beginnings, and shared responsibility using ethical AI as infrastructure — not as a substitute for human judgment.

Who needs ISR?

  • Nonprofits & civic groups seeking fair, smarter governance
  • Cooperatives & DAOs needing transparent and merit-based decisions
  • Organizations wanting to combine direct democracy with expert guidance
