Index of Social Responsibility (ISR)

Evidence-based democracy for communities, organizations, and DAOs.

What is ISR?

ISR is a platform that transforms group decision-making. Unlike one-person-one-vote systems, ISR calculates a dynamic influence score for each member, combining expertise, contribution, and contextual relevance to the voted issue. Every vote reflects the true wisdom and responsibility of your community.
