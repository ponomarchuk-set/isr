Values description:

I believe in the boundless potential of the human spirit. My vision for the future is a 'shining city on a hill'—green, yes, but green because of innovation, not stagnation. I see a community where people are free to dream, to build, and to solve problems without the heavy hand of bureaucracy.

I want a community rooted in faith, family, and freedom, where the government gets out of the way. Freedom is not just a political word; it is the engine of everything good. In the future community, the individual is king (or queen). Whether it's choosing a school for your kids or starting a business, the choice must be yours.

I believe true conservation comes from ownership, not restriction. We are stewards of this beautiful planet God gave us. Stewardship means passing on a world that is freer and more prosperous. We protect the vulnerable—the unborn, the elderly—because a society is judged by how it treats its weakest members.

I believe in the volunteer spirit. If there is a problem, we fix it together—family, church, neighbors. Sharing should be voluntary, an act of charity, not a mandate.

We need laws that are clear and enforced. A community needs a strong moral center. Stability comes from a solid foundation of values—honesty, hard work, patriotism. I believe that a community without faith is like a tree without roots. We need to acknowledge a higher power.

We should be proud of who we are and what we believe. Authenticity means living your values and not apologizing for them. When Over-Regulation conflicts with Economic Freedom, I choose Freedom. We will find a way to save the beetle and build the factory. When State Mandates conflict with Individual Liberty, I choose Liberty. When Radical Change attacks Traditional Values, I choose Tradition. New isn't always better. Sometimes the old ways are the best ways.
